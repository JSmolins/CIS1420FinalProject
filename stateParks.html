Park Etiquette

  1. Clean Up After Yourselves
    1. Place trash in proper recpticles
    2. Suffieciently put out your fires
    3. Clean up area before leaving
  2. Be Kind To Your Neighbors
    1. Keep noise levels to a minimum
    2. No harassment of any kind
  3. Stay Safe
    1. Don't forget sunscreen
    2. Bring sufficient water and snacks
    3. Wear proper footwear and attire
